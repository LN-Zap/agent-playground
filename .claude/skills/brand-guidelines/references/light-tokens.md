## Color Differences from Dark Mode

Key changes in light mode:
- **Background**: `#FCFCFC` (off-white) instead of `#000000` (black)
- **Primary text**: `#050505` (near-black) instead of `#FFFFFF` (white)
- **Accent color**: `#471414` (deep rose) instead of `#FFD4D4` (light rose)
- **Success color**: `#2E994A` (darker green) instead of `#42B852` (lighter green)
- **Layers invert**: Light grays instead of dark grays
